# Historical Geographies of Chicagoland

Funded through <a target="_blank" href="https://crocus-urban.org/">CROCUS</a> at the Argonne National Laboratory and a research award from the <a target="_blank" href="https://seekcommons.org/fellows.html">SEEKCommons Project</a>, I am collecting data on historical patterns of industrial land use and fossil fuel development in the Chicago area. These projects include (1) filling gaps in historical data on urban built environments by digitizing records held in local archives; (2) developing computer vision and machine learning methods to extract a wide range of fossil fuel-related sites from historical atlases; and (3) linking text analysis and spatial methodologies to develop new data on social-environmental change in cities.
